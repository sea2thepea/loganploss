PINS

People in Need of Supervision

Logan Ploss is the lead guitarist and vocalist of the group. Logan grew up in the capital region of New York, and started playing the trombone as his first instrument. He draws inspiration from other prolific guitarists and songwriters, namely Elliot Smith, Jimi Hendrix, and Johnny Greenwood.

Jacob Sullivan is a profuse(?) instrumentalist that is well-versed with multiple instruments (i think). However, his bass lines will have you dancing before you realize. He is originally from LOCATION and is majoring in MAJOR.

Vatressa Teamoh is an incredible drummer, and has a pocket that leaves the listener awe-struck at how funky she is. She is originally from LOCATION and is majoring in MAJOR.
